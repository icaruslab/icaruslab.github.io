Community

Icarus Project is an open source project that anyone in the community can use, improve, and enjoy. We’d love you to join us! Here’s a few ways to find out what’s happening and get involved.

Get in touch

The Icarus Project is nothing without its community. We think that constant communication with journalists, researchers and tech practitioners in a specific political context allows us to better know and respond to what is happening around us. As such, we would like to hear from you about your problems, your solutions, your thoughts on where to take this project. Get in touch with us so that you can become part of the Icarus Project.

info@fightcensorship.tech

Make Icarus Project documentation available in your language

Want to make the Icarus Project documentation guides available in your language? We would greatly appreciate the help to bring the project to the attention of other communities. If you hit a snag, we would be happy to go over any technical details of translation. And once things are done, feel free to reach out so that we can post the guides here on our site and on our social media.
